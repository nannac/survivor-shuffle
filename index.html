        How to Use Survivor Shuffle

        • Add Players: Use the form on the right to add new players.
        • Teams & Queue: Players are assigned to Team A, Team B, or the Queue based on wins and the team size.
        • Change Team Size: Select the desired team size from the dropdown. Teams will rebalance automatically.
        • Mark as Skip: Click "Skip" on a player to temporarily remove them from teams and the queue.
        • Record Results: Click "Team A Wins" or "Team B Wins" to record a match and update stats.
        • Remove Players: Click the ✕ next to a player to remove them from the app.

        All changes are local and not saved after closing the page.
